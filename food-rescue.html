Food Rescue

About Food Rescue

Our Food Rescue program is dedicated to saving surplus food from farms, markets, and restaurants and redistributing it to families and communities in need. Together, we combat food waste and hunger.

How It Works

Step 1: Collect surplus food from local partners.

Our dedicated team collaborates with restaurants, supermarkets, and local food producers to gather edible surplus food. This helps reduce food waste while addressing hunger.

Step 2: Sort and ensure food quality for redistribution.

The collected food is carefully sorted, checked for quality, and stored under hygienic conditions. We ensure only the best quality food is distributed to the beneficiaries.

Step 3: Deliver food to shelters, schools, and families in need.

Once sorted, the food is packed and delivered efficiently to ensure it reaches the people who need it most, including shelters, schools, and vulnerable communities.

Our Impact

Since the program's inception, we have achieved:

  • Saved 5 million kg of surplus food.
  • Fed 2 million people.
  • Partnered with 500+ organizations.
