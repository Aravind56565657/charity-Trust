Our Programs

Basic Literacy

Our Basic Literacy program is designed to provide foundational skills in reading, writing, and arithmetic for individuals of all ages. This program is ideal for those who have had limited access to formal education.

  • Learn to read and write in your local language.
  • Develop essential numeracy skills for daily life.
  • Build confidence to pursue further education or employment.

Duration: 6 months | Age Group: 12+ years

Computer Skills

Our Computer Skills program equips participants with basic and intermediate computer knowledge to thrive in a digital world. This program is perfect for students, job seekers, and small business owners looking to enhance their digital proficiency.

  • Master the use of Microsoft Office tools like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Learn internet basics, including email communication and online research.
  • Understand cybersecurity essentials for safe online interactions.

Duration: 3 months | Age Group: 15+ years
